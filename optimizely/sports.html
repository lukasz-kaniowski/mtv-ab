      Our innovative Sky Sports Day Pass gives you 24 hours access to six Sky Sports channels for £9.99. These are; Sky Sports 1-4, Sky Sports News and Sky Sports F1.


      Once you activate your Sky Sports Day Pass the clock will start running for 24 hours, this means you’re unable to pause and resume the pass at another time. When you buy a Sky Sports Day Pass you don’t have to activate it straight away, you also have the option to save it for later so you can use it when it best suits you. It’s worth noting that Interactive and Red Button services are unavailable with your Sky Sports Day Pass.

      If you have a Sky Sports Day Pass voucher that you want to use, you can apply this to your account by going to My Account and clicking on Apply Voucher. You’ll then see the entitlement forthe voucher you've applied > click OK, and you’ll be all set to go.

      To watch sports on NOW TV you’ll need a minimum broadband download speed of 2.5mbps. You’ll need to have one our supported devices and have an active Sky Sports Day Pass. If you’re reading this with the device you plan to watch NOW TV on, try our handy System Requirements Checker, which will let you know if your current device is compatible.


      Top Tip: You don’t need to have a Sky Movies Month Pass or an Entertainment Month Pass subscription to watch movies on NOW TV.
